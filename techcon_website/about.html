History

TechCon began in 1989 in Accra, founded by John Smithson, Lisa Chen, and Raj Patel. What started as a modest gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts has evolved into a global platform for innovation, collaboration, and education. The early years focused on personal computing and internet technologies, but by the late 1990s, TechCon had introduced the Innovator Awards and expanded internationally.

Mission

Our mission is to empower innovators, foster cross-sector collaboration, and democratize access to technology. It serves as a bridge between startups, researchers, investors, and industry leaders, creating a vibrant ecosystem where bold ideas become market-ready solutions.
